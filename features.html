Key Features of ZenFlow

Intelligent Task Management

Organize your tasks with smart categories, due dates, and reminders. Prioritize what truly matters with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

Our intelligent system learns your work habits to suggest optimal times for tasks and helps you break down large projects into manageable steps. Collaborate with teams seamlessly by sharing task lists and assigning responsibilities. Stay on track with customizable notifications that adapt to your workflow.

Mindful Focus Timer

Utilize built-in Pomodoro timers with calming ambient sounds to enhance concentration and reduce distractions. Get into deep work effortlessly.

Choose from a variety of ambient sounds, customize your work and break intervals, and track your focus sessions to identify your most productive times. Our timer integrates seamlessly with task management, allowing you to start a focus session directly from a selected task.

Progress Tracking & Insights

Visualize your achievements with detailed reports and analytics. Understand your work patterns and celebrate your productivity milestones.

Gain valuable insights into your productivity trends with daily, weekly, and monthly reports. See how you're allocating your time across different projects and identify areas for improvement. Celebrate your successes with visual progress charts and gamified achievements.

Personalized Wellness Reminders

Integrate short breaks, hydration reminders, and gentle stretches into your workflow to ensure a balanced and healthy routine throughout your day.

Customize your wellness reminders to fit your schedule and preferences. Choose from a range of gentle nudges to take breaks, hydrate, and perform simple exercises. Prioritizing your well-being directly contributes to sustained focus and productivity.
